Important Info — Please Read

This calculator estimates your daily calorie target using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, one of the more reliable field equations for resting metabolic rate in healthy adults. It is still an estimate, not a metabolic test.

Your macros use a protein-forward target, fat within common healthy intake ranges, and carbohydrates for the remaining calories. Your numbers are a starting point, not a prescription. Adjust based on how your body and energy actually respond over 2–4 weeks.

This is not medical advice. Please work with a licensed physician or registered dietitian before adjusting your nutrition if any of the following apply:

  • You are pregnant or breastfeeding
  • You are under 18 or over 65
  • You have a current or past history of disordered eating
  • You are managing a medical condition — diabetes, thyroid disorders, PCOS, kidney or heart disease, etc.
  • You are taking medications that affect metabolism, appetite, or hormones
  • You are recovering from illness, injury, or surgery

By using this tool, you agree that any decisions you make based on these numbers are your own responsibility. This calculator is for informational and educational purposes only. It does not replace personalized guidance from a qualified healthcare professional.
